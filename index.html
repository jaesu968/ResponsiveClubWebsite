Magic Rulez Club

About Us

We are a passionate group of Magic The Gathering players, collectors, and overall enthusiasts.

If you like or love Magic the Gathering, like we do, this is the place for you.

Regular Events

Card Sorting

Sorting cards competition. We have a lot of people who need to sort their collection. Come help out and maybe trade some cards while you are at it.

Casual Play (Standard, Modern, Commander, etc.)

We have a lot of people who like to play all kinds of formats. Bring whatever deck you have and find a play group to join.

Draft Night

We also host a weekly draft night where everyone gets to draft a new deck each time they play. We run regular booster drafts and chaos drafts.
